The Beginning...

After senior high school, I veered off the path to becoming a professional accountant and transformed into a sleepless young enthusiast, very passionate and eager to know about the world of technology.
I had zero knowledge on how to use computers. I could only dream about all the things I wanted to do with programming if I became one. Without a laptop to try out the code examples from the books I read, it was only the thoughts of the things I wanted to do that kept me going.
Fast forward, it was time to apply for undergraduate degree course. Accounting was my preferred course but if I got admitted into a computer science degree course then it would be a requirement for my parents to get me laptop. I took my chances and that was how I got my first laptop which marks the beginning of my journey into the tech world.

April, 2020 - January, 2021
